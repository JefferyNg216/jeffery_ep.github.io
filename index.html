Journey on MSc of Data Science

About Me

I am Jeffery Ng, my goal is to be a Data Scientist and hope to learn more varieties of data models and methodologies from the MSc programme, so as to strengthen myself. I am currently working as a Data Analyst under Business Intelligence (BI) Team in a banking regulators, our team handle the data that return from licensed bank, my major responsibilities are:

  • Establish and improves data solutions that enable smooth data delivery and best fit to the business requirements
  • Review dependency and determines data structure towards changes and service request
  • Review, resolve and optimise the issues of ETL process, data warehouse, data mart and reporting
  • Investigate master / metadata to identify potential issues of ETL and data accuracy and propose adequate solutions
  • Implementing standard SDLC approach

    • My working experience utilises 80% of PL/SQL and 20% of python on coding tasks. Apart from this, I am enthusiastic about the design of database, data pipeline and data analysing, therefore, I build a tiny db system (PSQL) on my home NAS, which to handle the automation of data scraping, cleaning and transformation tasks with python, and store it for further analysis and visualisation.
