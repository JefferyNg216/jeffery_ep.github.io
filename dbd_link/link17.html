Indexing and Slicing Columns & Rows

DOT and Bracket method
The DOT method is good to find specific elements. The bracket method is intuitive and easy to follow. In this method, you can access the data by the generic name/header of the column.

Indexing and slicing rows in a DataFrame can also be done using following methods: Label-based 'loc' method / Index based 'iloc' method

import numpy as np
import pandas as pd

matrix_data = np.random.randint(1,10,size=20).reshape(5,4)
row_labels = ['A','B','C','D','E']
column_headings = ['W','X','Y','Z']
df = pd.DataFrame(data=matrix_data, index=row_labels,columns=column_headings)

#1. Create a new column using the following snippet:
print("\nA column is created by assigning it in relation\n",'-'*75, sep='')
'''Result =
A column is created by assigning it in relation
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
'''
df['New'] = df['X']+df['Z']
df['New (Sum of X and Z)'] = df['X']+df['Z']

print(df)
'''Result =
W X Y Z New New (Sum of X and Z)
A 6 6 2 2 8 8
B 9 3 8 6 9 9
C 2 4 9 7 11 11
D 3 1 2 3 4 4
E 3 6 2 9 15 15
'''

#2. Drop a column using the df.drop method:
print("\nA column is dropped by using df.drop() method\n",'-'*55, sep='')
'''Result =
A column is dropped by using df.drop() method
-------------------------------------------------------
'''

df = df.drop('New', axis=1) # Notice the axis=1 option, axis = 0 is #default, so one has to change it to 1

print(df)
'''Result =
W X Y Z New (Sum of X and Z)
A 6 6 2 2 8
B 9 3 8 6 9
C 2 4 9 7 11
D 3 1 2 3 4
E 3 6 2 9 15
''

#3. Drop a specific row using the df.drop method:
df1=df.drop('A')
print("\nA row is dropped by using df.drop method and axis=0\n",'-'*65, sep='')
'''Result =
A row is dropped by using df.drop method and axis=0
-----------------------------------------------------------------
'''

print(df1)
'''Result =
W X Y Z New (Sum of X and Z)
B 9 3 8 6 9
C 2 4 9 7 11
D 3 1 2 3 4
E 3 6 2 9 15
'''

#4. Change the original DataFrame by setting the inplace argument to True:
print("\nAn in-place change can be done by making inplace=True in the drop method\n",'-'*75, sep='')
'''Result =
An in-place change can be done by making inplace=True in the drop method
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
''

df.drop('New (Sum of X and Z)', axis=1, inplace=True)

print(df)
''Result =
W X Y Z
A 6 6 2 2
B 9 3 8 6
C 2 4 9 7
D 3 1 2 3
E 3 6 2 9
''
